The Problem

Exhausted Parents spending all day keeping their child safe

The Idea

Giving parents more time for themselves

The Solution

An app that alerts you when your child is in danger

Overview

SafeChild guarantee’s absolute protection of children, by alerting when child is in danger. It does this using a high tech band, which is stripped to the child. This watch senses and monitors the Child’s Patterns.

How It Works

First sign up and register into the app, and connect you phone to the wrist band of the child, then set up your profile, by providing us with information about yourself and child.

Child care app then provides with information about the general wellbeing of you child

Finally update your notification setting to the sound you prefer because SafeChild app will ring aloud and pop up alerts when you child is in danger.
