The Problem

Exhausted Parents spending all day keeping their child safe

The Idea

Giving parents more time for themselves

The Solution

An app that alerts you when your child is in danger

Overview

SafeChild App is built with high-tech sensors that detects any form of danger within a distance of 20 - 100metres; It provides you or your child's guardian with up-to-date information about the general wellbeing of you child.
What if you have more than one child..? We got you! Kindly place an order for extra bands and connect with the same app

How It Works

First, you have to sign up, and connect your phone to the wrist band of the child. Don't bother about battery durabilty, The wrist band is powered by solar panels

Set up your profile, by providing us with information about yourself and child. This would give you more control, and also help us provide you with better service

Finally, set your notification setting and volume to your preference. SafeChild App rings out loudly by default, and pop up alerts when your child is in danger.
