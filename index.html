Overview

SafeChild App works with a high-tech sensored wrist watch (SafeChild Watcher - SCW) that detects any form of danger from a distance of 20 - 100metres; It provides you or your child's guardian with up-to-date information about the general wellbeing of you child.

What if you have more than one child..? We got you! This app can connect to multiple SafeChild Watchers

The Problem

Exhausted Parents spending all day keeping their child safe

The Idea

Giving parents more time for themselves

The Solution

An app that alerts you when your child is in danger

How It Works

First, you have to sign up, and connect your phone to the SCW placed on the wrist of the child. Don't bother about battery durabilty, The wrist band is powered by solar panels

Set up your profile, by providing us with information about yourself and child. This would give you more control, and also help us provide you with better service

Finally, set your notification setting and volume to your preference. SafeChild App rings out loudly by default, and pop up alerts when your child is in danger.
How easy is that!
